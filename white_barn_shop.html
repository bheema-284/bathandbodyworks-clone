About the White Barn Shop from Bath & Body Works

Bring home fragrance with a touch of elegance. The White Barn Shop is the place for beautifully fragrant (and gorgeous) candles, hand soaps, plug-in air fresheners and more.

Our 3-Wick Candles (in each and every sophisticated White Barn fragrance) are the absolute best for a reason – well, five reasons:

Made with essential oils
Beautiful room-filling fragrances
Amazing quality from beginning to end
Every candle is 100% guaranteed

Another reason we think you’ll love White Barn candles? They come in so many gorgeous designs and a rainbow of colors to fit (or enhance!) literally any space and style. P.S. don’t forget to add a super-chic candle holder for the finishing touch.

By the way, you can have effortless home fragrance every day…refresh any time with Wallflowers refills. When paired with a Bath & Body Works Wallflowers plug, the scented-oil refill will keep your home fresh for weeks (and weeks) with non-stop fragrance. And for when you’re having last-minute guests over: room sprays. Room sprays. Room sprays! In just two quick bursts, the room will be instantly revitalized.

And we didn’t forget our sudsy White Barn goodies – our soaps are perfect for every sink. The gentle foaming hand soaps come in every fragrance you’d ever want (floral, spicy, clean, etc.), plus they’re blended with nourishing vitamin E, shea extract and aloe to soften and cleanse hands.

Looking for more fragrances? Explore our new arrivals (in all the colors of the rainbow) right…here.
